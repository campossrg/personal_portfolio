Project link


T-Mobilitat contactless card integration

T-Mobilitat is the project for the integration of the conctactless card that unifies all the forms of transportion on Catalonia. The aim is to promote the unique and unified mode of payment as an electronic wallet that can be top-up at every point of sale. The system is managed by application servers interconnecting every transport operator. On each of them the different modules administrate the diverse list of funcionalities: Transport topology layer, field equipments, transport tickets, fares, sending of configurations and action lists, etc.
